Exam Preparation Tips

Plan Your Time

Use a study planner to allocate time for each subject. Stick to a routine for consistency.

Practice and Revise

Attempt past papers and revise key concepts to build confidence.

Focus on Health

Maintain a balanced diet and get enough sleep to keep your mind sharp.

Effective Study Techniques

Pomodoro Method

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat for maximum focus.

Chunking

Divide large topics into smaller, manageable sections for better understanding.

Visualization

Create charts or diagrams to remember complex information.
